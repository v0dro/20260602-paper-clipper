Account Deletion Request

Paper Clipper · Data & account removal

Paper Clipper stores your clippings locally on your device — uninstalling the app or clearing its data already removes everything on your phone. This form is for deleting server-side records (such as a sign-in identifier or proxy usage logs) tied to your account.

Want to delete what’s on your phone? Open the app and delete the clippings you want to remove, or uninstall the app to erase everything stored locally. Use this form only if you also want server-side records removed.

Request deletion

Enter the username associated with your account. Submitting the form will open your email client with the request pre-filled, addressed to the developer.

The username you use to sign in to Paper Clipper.

What happens next

  • Your email client opens with a pre-filled message to the developer.
  • Send the email to complete the request.
  • Server-side records tied to the username are deleted, typically within 30 days.
  • You’ll receive a confirmation reply once the deletion is done.
